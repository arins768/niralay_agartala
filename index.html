About Niralay

Niralay Homestay is nestled on Narshingarh PTC Road, Agartala, Tripura. With 4.5+ stars on Google and 4.4 on MakeMyTrip, we offer ten spacious (10×12 ft) rooms, each featuring a king-size bed, attached kitchen & washroom, 24×7 internet, AC, continuous gas, and solar-powered backup. Check-in from 9:30 AM and check-out by 11:00 AM.
